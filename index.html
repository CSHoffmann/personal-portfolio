NYC Rent Explorer

NYC Explorere is an interactive, web-based dashboard used to analyze NYC rent data. The user can explore average rent price of apartments filtered by neighborhood and borough location, number of rooms, and the year. The application also inclueds price time series and forcasting, and distributional visualizations of rent prices.

Technologies Used: R, Shiny (R package), Agile development process and Git version control.
